Innovations

Through years of innovative thinking our engineers were able to come up with some of the best ideas that the transportation industry has ever seen.

The entire fleet operates with electricity, no fossil fuels are required. With the advances in renewable energy sources in the last few years, the whole grid is powered by solar panels, windmills, hydroenergy, geothermal, etc.

Each vehicle has a set of electrochemical cells that converts the chemical energy of hydrogen and oxygen into electricity through a pair of redox reactions.

These fuel cells are different from most batteries in requiring a continuous source of oxygen (usually from air) to sustain the chemical reaction, whereas in a battery the chemical energy usually comes from metals and their ions or oxides that are commonly already present in the battery.

In total, the US transportation sector—which includes cars, trucks, planes, trains, ships, and freight—produces nearly thirty percent of all US global warming emissions, more than almost any other sector.

Exortus fleet does not need fossil fuels at any stage of the process. By not burning fuel there are no emissions. This helps to slow down the pace of global warming.

Not using tires helps to preserve the environment as well, while ensuring maximum efficiency by eliminating the road friction factor.

Efficiency has always been one of our main priorities, that is why in our latest iteration, photovoltaic cells were removed from the vehicles and istead were installed in the charging stations.

Solar power pannels are heavy, inneficient in vehicles that sometimes park under a tree or covered garages, and very often not sustainable. The weight they add to the vehicle is not compensated by the amount of energy they produce.

In terms of efficiency it makes more sense to install solar pannels on every charging station, make these stations widely available and use the integrated technologies to monitor battery levels on each vehicle.

The reliability of the 5G network allows the system to be always connected. Navigation systems can always count with finding the best routes to any destination.

This technology also allows for precise management of supply and demand for vehicles, real time data updates informs where more vehicles are required for service while keeping at bay excess of vehicles suppy.

Self driving capabilities have been implemented to the maximum extent of specialized artificial intelligence advancements and the 5G technology helps to create a seamless experience.
