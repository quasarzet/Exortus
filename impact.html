Since our Magnetic Levitation technology uses electricity from renewable sources, gasoline or diesel is not required at any point of the fueling process. This saves a tremendous amount of carbon monoxide from being dumped onto the athmosphere.

Microplastics are very small pieces of plastic that pollute the environment. There is no accepted standard for sample collection, processing, analysis and reporting of these pollutants, According to a study from the San Francisco Bay Microplastics Project in 2019, the biggest source of microplastic pollution in California’s coastal waters may come from car tires. Hovering a few centimeters above the surface allows our vehicles to discard tires and adopt an electronic suspension system anytime the vehicles need to land on the ground.

Most conventional vehicles even if electric, use standard brake pads made from copper, steel, graphite, and brass bonded with resin. A study from King's College London in 2016 determined that some roads where the air pollution benefits from improvements in diesel exhausts were outweighed by increases in particles that come from the wear of tyres, brakes and the road. Exortus vehicles use magnetic fields that push and pull the vehicles by alternating electric currents that are inoffensive to the environment.
